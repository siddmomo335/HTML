Inside Out

A simple fan page for the animated film

Story Characters Themes

Story

Inside Out follows the story of Riley, an eleven-year-old girl who moves to a new city. The film shows her emotions as characters inside her mind.

Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear and Disgust must work together to guide Riley through a difficult time in her life.

Main Characters

  • Joy - The optimistic leader of Riley's emotions.
  • Sadness - The thoughtful emotion who teaches Riley to feel deeply.
  • Anger - Quick to react and always ready to protect Riley.
  • Fear - Cautious and careful, keeping Riley safe from danger.
  • Disgust - Keeps Riley from harm by avoiding unpleasant situations.

Themes

The main themes are growing up, emotional balance, and the importance of feeling all emotions.

Inside Out shows that sadness is as necessary as joy, and that the full range of feelings helps us understand ourselves.
